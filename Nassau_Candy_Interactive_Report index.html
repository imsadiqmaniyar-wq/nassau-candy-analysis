02

05

Qualify a backup manufacturer for the top-5 Chocolate SKUs to reduce factory concentration risk. This secures the 94%+ of revenue currently dependent on just two production sites.

10 — Conclusion

Final Assessment

A business with elite fundamentals and a clear strategic mandate: protect the Chocolate core while systematically reducing concentration risk.

Analyst Verdict

Nassau Candy Distributor is a high-margin, Chocolate-led business with strong fundamentals and clear growth levers. The primary task is not revenue growth per se, but systematic risk reduction through product, geographic, and supply chain diversification — while protecting the elite margin profile of the Chocolate core.

💪
Strengths 65.91% average gross margin · 100% clean data · Extraordinary Chocolate margin stability (±0.18pp) · Strong top-5 product cluster with 67%+ margins consistently maintained over 25 months.
🎯
Risks to Address 5 SKUs = 80% revenue · 2 factories = 94% revenue · 3 states = 40% revenue · Kazookles at 7.7% GM · Other division at 44.84% margin dragging portfolio averages.
